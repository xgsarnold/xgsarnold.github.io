About Me

I'm a deverloper with a master's degree from Duke University in Religion and Social Theory. These studies cultivated my passion for problem-solving and taught me to clearly communicate while attuned to the concerns of others. After nearly ten years of studying the humanities I decided to pursue my other passion: technology.

I've recently completed an intensive 12-week software development camp at the Iron Yard. In the backend course I learned development with Ruby on Rails, SQL, JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX, HTML, and CSS. With a focus on production we used Heroku as well as tools like S3, numerous GEMs, test-driven and Agile development.

I was drawn to coding, because of my love for creativity, innovative problem-solving, and dedication to a craft. Coding satisfies these thirsts while promising to immerse one in new technological developments of the world. I'm eager to continue learning and expanding into new languages and technologies. I also desire strong mentoring to guide my growth as a developer.
